I am a lecturer in the Informatics Department at the UIN Sunan Gunung Djati Bandung. I am passionate about creating open, cheap, and robust geospatial platforms. I have six years of experience as a GIS developer and data engineer in the West Java provincial government (48 million population), focusing on poverty reduction, regional resource management, One Spatial Data policy, and the Covid-19 task force.
