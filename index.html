Ideas To Be Realised

On Body and Mind

Integrated intelligent systems that can help people achieve their dreams, such as a wearable exoskeleton connected to non-invasive body signal recognisers, which not only physically guides us out of our laziness by prompting our body to make the necessary movements to achieve our specific life goals, but also reads our current mental state to provide interactive responses that motivate us based on our condition

On World Peace

An integrated digital media platform that spreads positive trends, values, and inspirations, promoting peace, kindness, empathy, and tolerance worldwide, with a primary focus on emerging youth leaders in nuclear-armed countries, and at some point in time, people will realise, and without any war happening, every country will begin to convert their warheads into abundant sources of electricity

On Self-Sustaining Food

A compact automatic machine that is small enough to be installed in a small house or apartment unit, and can automatically grow, harvest, and process food sufficient to meet more than half of the inhabitants' needs. This machine would likely leverage exceptionally fast-growing food sources such as fungi, insects, microgreens, algae, or other options, making it deployable in famine-stricken regions around the world. Imagine receiving a fungi-based or insect-based patty for your burger, automatically prepared and packed every day for free

On Microeconomic Growth

Sustainable income growth, achieved through high-quality goods and services, is a key driving factor in combating poverty. By utilising localized big data, this intelligent system will guide you in selecting the right business, identifying target customers, sourcing product components, building a marketable product, designing training courses for workers, and creating effective campaigns. Ultimately, it will transform individuals into sociopreneurs, with the goal of accelerating labor absorption and alleviating poverty
