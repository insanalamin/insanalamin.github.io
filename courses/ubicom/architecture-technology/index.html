## Contoh Arsitektur Sistem

  subgraph T0[Things]
    T1[Microcontroller: Arduino / ESP]

    T4[Multimedia Sensors: Camera / Mic / etc.]
    T2 --> T1
    T1 --> T3
  end

    E3[Event Responder: Node-RED]

    E2 <--> E3
    E3 <--> E4
  end

    C2[Web service: Python]
    C3[Scalable Database]

    C2 <--> C3
    C4 <--> C5
    C2 --> C5

    C6 <--> C3
    C5 <--> C7

  end
